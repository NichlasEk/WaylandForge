# stormakt-oresund-armored-train-v1

Generation mode: built-in image generation followed by the imagegen skill's chroma-key removal helper.

Prompt:

> Use case: stylized-concept. Asset type: production sprite sheet for the retro 1990s dark Scandinavian science-fantasy top-down shoot-em-up Stormakt 3020. Create exactly four large isolated armored railway subjects in a clean 2x2 grid on a perfectly flat solid #ff00ff chroma-key background. Strict top-down / slightly elevated orthographic game view; every vehicle is oriented vertically with its front pointing toward the top of the canvas. Generous uniform magenta gutters, no overlap, no cast shadows, no floor, no text, no UI, no ships, no people. Top-left: a Swedish-Danish orbital armored locomotive, long black gunmetal boiler, crown-shaped prow, riveted blue-black armor, brass steam machinery, tiny cold cyan lamps. Top-right: a matching heavy cannon wagon with a clearly readable rotating broadside turret and red-white Danish warning panels. Bottom-left: a matching armored command/cargo wagon with a conspicuous small golden mechanical coupling at its upper end. Bottom-right: the same cannon wagon wrecked sideways against a railway buffer, torn but compact, orange embers and bent iron contained entirely within the quadrant. Gritty painterly pre-rendered pixel-art texture, 1990s PC game aesthetic, coherent scale and lighting across all four cells, readable when reduced to 35-90 pixels. The #ff00ff background must remain perfectly uniform with no gradients, texture, reflection or lighting variation, and #ff00ff must not appear in the subjects.

The untouched chroma generation is preserved as `stormakt-oresund-armored-train-v1-source.png`. It was converted with automatic border-key sampling, soft matte, transparent threshold `12`, opaque threshold `220` and despill. The packer selects the largest alpha component from each fixed quadrant before downsampling, preventing key residue or debris from leaking between sprites.

# RTS Danish army v1

Built-in image generation. Production 5x2 chroma-key animation atlas with ready and attack frames for toll stormer, ledger pikeman, coin mastiff, powder boar and organ-gun wagon. Grotesque 17th-century Danish oxblood-and-cream troops fused with black iron and tarnished brass, dark late-1990s strategy-game pixel art, orthographic top-down 3/4 view, uniform green background, no scenery, labels, shadows or loose fragments.

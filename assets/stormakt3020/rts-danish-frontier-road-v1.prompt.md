Built-in image generation, `stylized-concept` mode.

Final prompt: Create a two-cell, top-down RTS terrain sheet of a narrow 17th-century Danish military frontier road in a dark Swedish forest. Both vertical road segments must run seamlessly from top to bottom with identical width: one intact and one churned by battle. Use wet packed earth, dark cobbles, wagon ruts, boot marks, iron drainage grates, moss, and restrained red-white Danish border stakes. Render as detailed dark painterly pixel-art-inspired game art matching Stormakt 3020. Place the roads on perfectly flat `#ff00ff` chroma key outside their edges. No perspective, units, vehicles, buildings, text, watermark, bright colors, modern asphalt, or science-fiction objects.

The chroma source is retained alongside the alpha-clean production sheet.

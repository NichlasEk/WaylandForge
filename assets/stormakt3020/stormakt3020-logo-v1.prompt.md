# Stormakt 3020 menu logo v1

Generated with the built-in image generation tool on 2026-07-11, using the dark Swedish player frigate and Stora Bält environment sheet as material-language references.

The production asset `stormakt3020-logo-v1.png` was extracted from `stormakt3020-logo-v1-source.png` with the imagegen skill's border auto-key, soft matte, despill, transparent threshold 12 and opaque threshold 220. `tools/stormakt3020/build_assets.py` derives 210x105 wide and 150x75 legacy-resolution sprites for the WFSA pack.

## Prompt

Create a single wide title-logo bitmap asset for the game menu. Exact visible title text: “STORMAKT 3020”. The lettering must be perfectly legible, centered, monumental, compact and wide, like a premium late-1990s Japanese arcade shoot-em-up title rendered in a richly detailed modern pixel-art and painted-sprite hybrid. Build the letters from blackened steel and royal-blue enamel with restrained worn-gold edges. Place a clear Swedish three-crowns heraldic emblem above or integrated behind the center, with subtle symmetrical cannon, spear, laurel, and star-navy ornament framing the title. Grandiose, heroic, slightly mad futuristic Great Power era mood; dark and serious, not cute. Strong silhouette when reduced to about 220 pixels wide. No Danish red and white. No spaceships covering the title. No characters. No UI panel, buttons, menu options, border, watermark, subtitle, or text other than exactly STORMAKT 3020. Isolated asset on a perfectly flat solid chroma-key green background #00FF00, with no green reflections, no green inside the logo, no shadow beyond the asset. Landscape composition with clean green margin on all sides.

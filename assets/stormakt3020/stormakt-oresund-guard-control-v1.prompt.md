The production PNG was auto-keyed from the border with a soft matte, transparent threshold 12, opaque threshold 220, despill and forced alpha. The packer crops all four quadrants independently, retains each cell's largest alpha component and scales the two craft to at most 30x36 pixels and the two control houses to at most 38x34 pixels.

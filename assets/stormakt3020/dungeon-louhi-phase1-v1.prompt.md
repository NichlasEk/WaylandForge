## Exact final prompt

```text
Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: production 2x2 gameplay sprite sheet for Stormakt 3020, an isometric/top-down 1990s dark fantasy action game
Primary request: Create Louhi, Silverbergets häxa, as three coherent full-body boss animation poses plus her silver altar, arranged in an exact clean 2x2 grid.
Input images: Image 1 is the existing Louhi portrait and must define her identity, face, silver-streaked black hair, hornlike silver crown, navy-black clothing and swan armor; Image 2 is the existing Tuonela Swan boss sheet and defines gameplay sprite scale, rendering detail and 2x2-sheet structure; Image 3 is the temple prop sheet and defines isometric perspective, black iron, blue runes and antique silver-gold trim.
Scene/backdrop: perfectly flat solid #ff00ff chroma-key background, with a thin straight #ffffff divider at the exact horizontal and vertical center only.
Subject: top-left Louhi standing idle with a long black-silver rune staff; top-right Louhi aggressively casting with staff extended and three small silver rune sparks; bottom-left Louhi mid-teleport with cloak curling into a compact pool of black water but her body still clearly readable; bottom-right a broad ancient silver altar with a central heart-shaped silver core and swan-wing relief, no character.
Style/medium: crisp hand-painted pixel-art game sprites, late-1990s dark fantasy PC action RPG, realistic anatomy translated into dense readable pixels, matching the supplied assets rather than modern smooth concept art.
Composition/framing: one isolated centered asset per equal quadrant, generous padding, consistent Louhi scale and isometric three-quarter/front view, full body and staff entirely inside each cell.
Lighting/mood: cold cyan rune glow, restrained warm antique-gold details, solemn and threatening.
Color palette: black iron, gunmetal, dark navy, cold silver, pale cyan, tiny antique-gold accents. Do not use magenta in any asset.
Materials/textures: engraved silver, worn black steel, heavy wool and feathers, wet black water.
Constraints: preserve Louhi's recognizable face, crown, hair, blue-black costume and swan breastplate from the portrait; exactly four cells; top-left idle, top-right casting, bottom-left teleport, bottom-right altar; no overlap across divider; no cast shadow; no text; no UI; no border except the center divider.
Avoid: giant wings on Louhi, duplicate limbs, cropped staff, photorealism, soft painterly blur, bright fantasy colors, fire, green, extra characters, scenery, floor plane, gradients, shadows, reflections, watermark.
```

Prompt:

> Use case: stylized-concept. Create a production two-frame radio portrait sprite sheet for the game Stormakt 3020, directly inspired by the poised Swedish Carolean officer in the reference image but depicting an original fictional adult man, Karl CCLV, not the historical person. Preserve the reference's upright aristocratic posture, long narrow face, swept-back dark blond hair, royal-blue fitted military coat, gold cuffs and brass buttons. Make him about 35-45, battle-hardened, self-assured and slightly eccentric. Futurize the uniform into a retro-futuristic 3020 Carolean admiral: deep cobalt-blue armored wool coat, subtle dark steel collar plates, worn brass data clasps, small blue-yellow command insignia, one restrained cyan optical instrument at the high collar, no helmet, no hat, no weapons. Gritty pre-rendered 1990s PC game pixel-art texture, realistic painted face, dramatic dark teal radio lighting, head-and-upper-torso crop, facing three-quarters toward screen right, matching the existing Stormakt radio portraits. Exactly two equal square cells side by side on a perfectly uniform solid #ff00ff chroma-key background with a generous clean gutter. Left cell: neutral, mouth closed, skeptical raised eyebrow. Right cell: same exact person, pose, scale, costume and lighting, mouth naturally open mid-speech with a faint confident grin. No text, no UI, no frame, no extra people, no gradients or shadows in the background, no #ff00ff inside the subject. Keep both portraits wholly within their cells and centered consistently.

The runtime source is alpha-cleaned from the versioned chroma-key source and packed as `portrait_karl_neutral` and `portrait_karl_speak`.

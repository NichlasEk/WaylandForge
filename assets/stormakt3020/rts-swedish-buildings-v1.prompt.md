Built-in image generation. Production 4x2 chroma-key sprite atlas in the established dark late-1990s strategy-game style: steam plant idle/working, silver crusher idle/working, Carolean barracks, moose animal hall, defense tower idle/firing. Orthographic top-down 3/4 view, Swedish blue and muted yellow, aged brass and gunmetal, uniform magenta background, no scenery, labels, shadows or loose fragments.

# stormakt-oresund-background-v1

Generation mode: built-in image generation.

Prompt:

> Create a production-ready opaque vertical-scrolling shoot-em-up background plate for a retro 1990s dark Scandinavian science-fantasy game called Stormakt 3020. View is strict top-down / slightly elevated orthographic, designed for a narrow 400x875 gameplay field. The scene is the Öresund Ring Bridge in space: an immense Swedish-Danish orbital rail bridge crossing a black-blue void, weathered gunmetal deck, parallel railway lines, repeating riveted sleepers, gothic industrial cable towers and circular ring-bridge architecture, distant cold planet glow and sparse stars. Palette: charcoal, oxidized steel, muted cyan, tiny amber signal lights. Painterly pixel-art texture, gritty pre-rendered 1990s PC game aesthetic, readable at low resolution, no text, no logos, no UI, no ships, no characters, no projectiles. Keep the central flight corridor comparatively uncluttered and dark; detailed structural scenery mostly along the left and right edges. Seam-friendly vertical composition, opaque full-frame environment art.

The packer scales the source to legacy and wide vertical plates, mirrors each plate vertically and scrolls the result as a non-physical background.

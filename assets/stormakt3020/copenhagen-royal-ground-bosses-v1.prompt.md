# Copenhagen royal ground bosses v1

Generated with the built-in image generator on 2026-07-20. Both sources were generated on flat green and converted locally with soft matte, despill, transparent threshold 12 and opaque threshold 220. Runtime collision, damage windows and telegraphs remain code-owned.

The generated Saga King charge crossed the fourth cell's left boundary. The cleaned runtime sheet therefore isolates that connected alpha component, scales it proportionally to 348 pixels wide and centers it inside the 384-pixel cell with 18-pixel horizontal padding. The other seven cleaned cells remain unchanged.
